---
title: Twilio SMS Provider
subtitle: How to integrate MagicBell with Twilio for a magical SMS experience
---

To add SMS bases notifications to your project you will need to create a [Twilio](https//www.twilio.com) account. Once you've signed up for one, reach out to our support team with your Account SID and Account Secret and let us help set up your SMS notifications in no time.

The only thing that remains after that is to ensure you [add phone numbers to your MagicBell users](http://localhost:3000/docs/rest-api/reference#update-user).
